Project Overview

A final-year project built to make shopping smarter

PriceVerse brings together real-time web scraping, a structured product database, and an AI assistant into a single, modern web application. It continuously collects phone listings from multiple Pakistani retailers, normalizes them, and presents the best available price alongside discount insights and price trends.

The goal is simple: give shoppers a trustworthy, data-driven view of phone prices so they can compare confidently, spot genuine deals, and get notified the moment a price drops.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how PriceVerse works.

Which phones does PriceVerse track?
PriceVerse focuses on Samsung and Apple iPhone devices, building a structured database of models, specifications and live prices from multiple stores.
Where do the prices come from?
Prices are scraped directly from real retailers such as MobileTrade and PriceOye, so the figures you see reflect actual store listings rather than estimates.
How does the discount detection work?
Each listing stores its original and current price. PriceVerse compares them to compute the real discount percentage and highlights genuine sales while making inflated "fake" discounts easy to spot.
What can the AI chatbot do?
The assistant answers questions about phone prices, specifications, comparisons, discounts and recommendations — all based on the products in the PriceVerse database.
How do price drop alerts work?
When you tap “Notify Me” on a product, PriceVerse records your subscription and emails you when the best price drops or a better discount becomes available.
